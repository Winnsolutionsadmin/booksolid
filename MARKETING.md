## Partnership Strategy

### Priority Partnerships
1. **Vagaro / Mindbody certified consultants** — co-sell BookSolid as the "layer on top" of their existing systems
2. **Local chambers of commerce** (Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Rocklin, Placer County) — speaking + member discounts
3. **Massage therapy school placement offices** — BookSolid as the recommended tool for new practitioners
4. **Personal trainer certification programs** (ACE, NASM) — continuing education partnerships
5. **Small business accountants + bookkeepers** — referral program ($200/referral) for CPAs serving service businesses

### Referral Program Structure
- Client referral: $200 account credit per referred client (perpetual)
- Partner referral: 20% rev share first 6 months + $100 setup bonus
- Affiliate: 15% recurring commission for bloggers/content creators

### Local Business Outreach
- Sacramento Small Business Development Center (SBDC) — free workshop: "AI Tools That Pay for Themselves"
- SCORE Sacramento chapter — workshop + resource listing
- Placer County Economic Development — business resource directory

4. Day 3: Set up your waitlist
5. Day 5: No-show fee / deposit configuration

7. Day 10: "You've saved X appointments — here's what's next"

2. Day 7: "Quick question about your scheduling"
3. Day 30: "We released [new feature] since you left"
