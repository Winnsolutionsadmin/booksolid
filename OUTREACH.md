- Personalization variables: [Business Name], [APPTS/week], [AVG_VALUE], [CALCULATED_LOSS]
- Source prospect data from: Google Maps, Yelp, local chamber of commerce directories
- Lead trigger: Any business with Google reviews mentioning "no-shows" or "hard to book"
