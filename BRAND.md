# BookSolid — Brand Identity

## Core Identity
- **Product Name:** BookSolid
- **Domain:** booksolid.ai
- **Tagline:** "Every appointment. Every time."
- **Sub-tagline:** "Stop losing money to no-shows. BookSolid fills your calendar and keeps it full."
- **Category:** AI Scheduling + No-Show Prevention

## Brand Positioning
BookSolid is the anti-no-show weapon for appointment-based businesses. Not just scheduling software — an intelligent system that reminds, recovers, and rebounds so your calendar stays profitable.

**Core Promise:** A no-show-proof booking system that pays for itself the first time it fills a cancelled slot.

- **Hero Gradient:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #0a0f1e 0%, #1a0535 50%, #0a0f1e 100%)`
- **CTA Button:** `linear-gradient(90deg, #00d4c8, #7c3aed)`
- **Accent Glow:** `radial-gradient(ellipse at center, rgba(0,212,200,0.15), transparent 70%)`

## Logo Concept
- **Mark:** A calendar grid with a solid teal checkmark overlaid — communicating "confirmed booking"
- **Wordmark:** "BookSolid" — "Book" in Warm White, "Solid" in Electric Teal
- **Tagline lock-up:** Small caps, Slate color, wide letter-spacing
- **Vibe:** Clean, dependable, modern — not playful, not corporate

## Typography
- **Display:** Space Grotesk (bold/extrabold) — headlines, hero text
- **Body:** Inter — all body copy, UI elements
- **Accent:** JetBrains Mono — metrics, numbers, stats

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Direct. Data-driven. Empathetic to the problem.
- **Personality:** The reliable contractor who's always on time — no fluff, just results
- **Copy style:** Short sentences. Money language. Specific numbers.
- **Never:** Generic ("boost your business!"), corporate jargon, passive voice, filler adjectives

### Voice Examples
- ❌ "BookSolid leverages AI to enhance your scheduling workflows"
- ✅ "The average no-show costs you $127. BookSolid cuts that rate by 83%."

- ❌ "Streamline your appointment management experience"
- ✅ "Your calendar fills itself. You focus on the work."

## Target Customer
- **Who:** Owners of appointment-based service businesses (salons, medical/dental practices, personal trainers, tutors, massage therapists, chiropractors, law firms, financial advisors)
- **Size:** 1–50 employees
- **Pain point:** 10–30% no-show rate bleeding $2K–$15K/year in lost revenue
- **Sophistication:** Uses phone + maybe an old scheduling tool; has tried reminders but they're manual
- **Decision driver:** Math — show them the dollar amount they're losing

## Brand Story (30 seconds)
"Every appointment on your calendar is a revenue commitment. When a client no-shows, you can't get that time back. BookSolid changes the math — AI reminders, smart rebooking, instant deposit collection, and automatic waitlist fill-in. Your calendar doesn't just look full. It stays full."

## Competitive Differentiation
- vs. Calendly: BookSolid fights no-shows; Calendly just books them
- vs. Acuity: BookSolid adds AI follow-up and recovery automation
- vs. Jane/Mindbody: BookSolid is industry-agnostic and half the price
- **Our edge:** The no-show guarantee angle — "We fill the slot or you don't pay the overage"
