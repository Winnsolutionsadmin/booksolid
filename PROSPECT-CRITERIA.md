# BookSolid — Prospect Search Criteria
## Sacramento / Placer County Target Market

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Business Type:** Appointment-based service business
**Size:** 1–15 employees
**Revenue:** $100K–$2M annually
**Appointments:** 15–200/week
**No-show exposure:** Any business with 10%+ no-show rate
**Tech posture:** Has a Google Business Profile; uses a phone for bookings or a basic scheduling tool
**Decision maker:** Owner or office manager with P&L awareness

**Disqualifiers:**
- Walk-in only (barbershops with no scheduling)
- Pure e-commerce (no appointments)
- Enterprise chains with central scheduling teams (>50 employees)
- Already using BookSolid-equivalent with high satisfaction

---

## Vertical Segments — Search Criteria

### Tier 1: Highest Pain, Best Fit

**1. Personal Trainers / Fitness Studios**
- Search: "personal trainer [city]" on Google Maps
- Google Maps filters: Personal trainer, Fitness center, Pilates studio, Yoga studio
- Target cities: Sacramento, Roseville, Rocklin, Folsom, Elk Grove, Lincoln, Auburn, Granite Bay
- Signal: 3+ Google reviews, active Google Business profile
- Red flag: Part of large franchise (LA Fitness, etc.)
- Est. count: 800–1,200 in region

**2. Massage Therapists / Spas**
- Search: "massage therapy [city]" or "day spa [city]"
- Google Maps: Massage therapist, Massage spa, Day spa
- Same cities as above
- Signal: 10+ reviews, booking link in profile, solo or small group practice
- Est. count: 600–900

**3. Chiropractors / Physical Therapists**
- Search: "chiropractor [city]", "physical therapist [city]"
- NPI registry lookup for solo/small practices
- CAQH registry (licensed practitioners)
- Signal: Solo or 2–3 practitioner office
- Est. count: 400–600

**4. Mental Health Therapists / Counselors**
- Search: Psychology Today provider directory, TherapyDen, ZocDoc
- Target: Solo practitioners, group practices <5 clinicians
- Signal: Accepting new clients, private pay accepted
- Est. count: 500–700

### Tier 2: High Value, Moderate Fit

**5. Hair Salons / Beauty Studios**
- Search: "hair salon [city]", "hair stylist [city]"
- Google Maps: Hair salon, Hairdresser
- Vagaro / StyleSeat / Booksy listings = pre-qualified (already paying for scheduling)
- Signal: 20+ reviews, booth rental or independent studio
- Est. count: 1,200–1,800

**6. Financial Advisors / Insurance Agents**
- FINRA BrokerCheck for independent advisors
- Search: "financial advisor [city]", "insurance agent [city]"
- Signal: Independent RIA, small broker-dealer, <5 advisors
- High appointment value ($200–500/visit) = high ROI
- Est. count: 300–500

**7. Tutors / Tutoring Centers**
- Search: "tutoring [city]", "SAT prep [city]"
- Wyzant / TutorMe listings
- Signal: Active during school year, 10+ sessions/week
- Est. count: 300–500

### Tier 3: Worth Testing

**8. Acupuncture / Alternative Medicine**
- Search: "acupuncture [city]"
- Est. count: 150–250

**9. Tattoo Artists / Studios**
- Search: "tattoo shop [city]"
- Signal: By-appointment only policy in reviews or bio
- Est. count: 100–200

**10. Home Service Pros (high-ticket)**
- HVAC, plumbing, electricians who take scheduled service calls
- Search: [service] + [city] with "schedule a visit" language on site
- Est. count: 200–400

## Data Sources for Prospecting

### Primary Sources
1. **Google Maps / Google Business Profiles**
   - Query: `site:maps.google.com "[profession]" "[city] CA"`
   - Or use Google Maps API for bulk extraction
   - Fields to capture: Business name, phone, website, review count, rating, category

2. **Yelp Business Listings**
   - Filter: Category → [profession], Location → Sacramento metro
   - Paid profiles = higher intent / more established

3. **LinkedIn**
   - Search: Job title "owner" OR "practice manager" + industry + location
   - Company size: 1–10 employees
   - Use Sales Navigator for bulk

4. **Vagaro / MindBody / Acuity / Booksy Provider Directories**
   - These businesses already pay for scheduling = pre-qualified pain
   - Extract via provider search pages

5. **Chamber of Commerce Directories**
   - Sacramento Metro Chamber: metrochamber.org
   - Roseville Chamber: rosevillechamber.com
   - Folsom Chamber: folsomchamber.com
   - Placer County EDC: placer.ca.gov/edc

### Secondary Sources
6. **NPI Registry** (for healthcare: therapists, chiropractors, PTs)
   - nppes.cms.hhs.gov — filter by state, county, specialty
7. **State Licensing Boards** (massage therapy, cosmetology)
   - California Board of Barbering and Cosmetology: search.dca.ca.gov
8. **Google Ads Data** (competitor keywords to find active searchers)

## Qualification Criteria (Score 1–5, Must Hit 12+)

| Criterion | Score | Why It Matters |
|-----------|-------|---------------|
| Appointment-based only | 1–3 | Core product fit |
| 10+ appointments/week | 1–3 | Enough volume to feel the pain |
| Has Google Business Profile | 1 | Signals basic online presence |
| 3.5–4.5 stars (reviews mentioning scheduling) | 1–2 | Pain signal in public data |
| Solo or small team (<10) | 1 | Owner is decision maker |
| No existing high-quality scheduling tool | 1–2 | Lower switching friction |
| Located in target market area | 1 | Enables local sales touch |
| Review text mentions "hard to book" or "no-show" | 2 | Direct pain evidence |

**Score 12–16: Priority outreach (Day 1)**
**Score 8–11: Standard sequence (Day 3)**
**Score <8: Lower priority or skip**
